Norwegian Potato Klubb

A hearty Norwegian dumpling made with potatoes and flour, often enjoyed with butter and salt.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups grated raw potatoes
  • 1 cup grated cooked potatoes
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • Â½ teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • Diced ham (optional)

Directions

  1. Mix together the raw potatoes, cooked potatoes, salt, baking powder, and flour to form a dough.
  2. Divide into portions and form into balls. If desired, put chunks of ham in the center of each portion.
  3. Drop dumplings into a large pot of simmering salted water.
  4. Cook for 30 to 40 minutes, until the dumplings are firm and cooked through.
  5. Serve hot with butter, salt, or side dishes of your choice.

Tips

Klubb tastes best when fresh, but leftovers can be sliced and pan-fried in butter the next day.
