Potato Klubb Recipe

Norwegian Potato Klubb

A hearty Norwegian dumpling made with potatoes and flour, often enjoyed with butter and salt.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups grated raw potatoes
  • 1 cup graded cooked potatoes
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • Diced ham

Directions

  1. Mix together the raw potatoes, cooked potatoes, salt, baking powder, and flour to form a dough.
  2. Divide into portions and form into balls. If desired, put chunks of ham in the mixture of each portion.
  3. Drop dumplings into a large pot of simmering salt water.
  4. Cook for 30 to 40 minutes, until the dumplings are firm and cooked through.
  5. Serve hot with butter, salt, or side dishes of your choice.

Tips

Klub tastes best when fresh, but leftovers can be sliced and pan-fried in butter the next day.
