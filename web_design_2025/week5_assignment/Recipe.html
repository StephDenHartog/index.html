Norwegian Potato Klub

A hearty Norwegian dumpling made with potatoes and flours, often enjoyed with butter and salt.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups grated raw potatoes
  • 1 cup graded cooded potatoes
  • 2 teasponns salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking poweder
  • 2 cup all-purpose flour
  • Diced ham
